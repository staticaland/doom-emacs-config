* Web Mode

I used this while making [[https://pkg.go.dev/html/template][Go HTTP templates]] while reading [[https://lets-go.alexedwards.net/][Let's Go]].

#+begin_src elisp
(use-package! web-mode
  :mode (("\\.html$" . web-mode)
         ("\\.tmpl\\'" . web-mode)))
#+end_src

#+begin_src elisp
(add-hook! web-mode
  (prettier-on-save-mode))
#+end_src
